Desired Airflow Rate: 0.5m/sec

Door Size: 41cm x 32cm

0.41 x 0.32 x 0.5 = 0.0656 m3/sec

0.0656 * 60 = 3.936 m3/min
0.0656 * 60 * 60 = 236.16 m3/h

In Japan is different, airflow is usually specified m3/minute.
